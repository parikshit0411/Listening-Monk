Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about purchasing Karma Coins

How do Karma Coins work?

Karma Coins are our virtual currency used to tip listeners and access premium features. Each coin represents a small monetary value that goes directly to supporting our compassionate community.

Do Karma Coins expire?

No, your Karma Coins never expire. Once purchased, they remain in your account indefinitely until you choose to use them.

Can I get a refund?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on unused Karma Coins. If you're not satisfied, contact our support team for a full refund.

Is my payment secure?

Absolutely. We use Stripe for payment processing, which employs bank-level security and encryption. Your payment information is never stored on our servers.

How do I tip a listener?

After a conversation, you'll have the option to tip your listener using your Karma Coins. Simply select the amount you'd like to give as appreciation for their time and support.

What are the premium features?

Premium features include priority matching with listeners, extended conversation time, access to specialized listeners, and detailed conversation insights.
