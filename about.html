Our Story

Founded on the principle that human connection and empathy can heal, Listening Monk was created to bridge the gap between those who need support and those willing to provide it. We've facilitated over 1 million meaningful conversations and continue to grow our community of listeners and seekers.

Every conversation on our platform is an opportunity for healing, growth, and human connection. Whether you're facing a difficult day, processing major life changes, or simply need someone to listen, our monks are here for you.
